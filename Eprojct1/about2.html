About Us

Meet our team


hi, i'm Thinh

VERY PLEASED TO MEET YOU!

It’s my joy to foster inspiration through the art
of pointed-pen calligraphy. With more than 20
years of calligraphy experience, I have been
serving wedding clients with my wide-range of
handwritten scripts as well as stationery design &
print production services.

Additionally, our small family business creates
educational resources and curates top notch
calligraphy supplies for those interested in
learning the art. We believe calligraphy should be
approachable so that others can flourish in their
creativity and inject a little bit of fancy into the
world through the art of pointed-pen.


hi, i'm Nhan

VERY NICE TO MEET YOU!

Calligraphy has been serving up handwritten
scripts since 2002 for countless couples, corporate clients,
and even a celeb or two (yes, stars: they love calligraphy just
like us!), handcrafting everything from addressed envelopes to
stationery design & print production to custom signage, all
from our Old Town Alexandria, Virginia studio.

Laura has personally trained under three amazing master
penman to date; Michael Sull, Vivian Mungall, and Harvest
Crittenden. She is an active member of IAMPETH & certified
instructor of American Cursive Handwriting.

Laura’s sister Alyssa came on as Director of Operations a few
years ago, allowing us to take our family business around the
world with online video courses, in-person workshops, and
our much-loved Calligraphy Starter Kit. More than 15,000
new calligraphers are out in the world now thanks to our little
shop & educational resources — a fun fact we think our parents should put on the refrigerator!
